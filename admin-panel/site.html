    Our Story

    Born for
    the Game

    Hamriyah Cricket Centre is Sharjah's most distinguished cricket venue — a ground born from passion and built for performance. Nestled in the heart of Hamriyah, our facility has been the launchpad for countless careers and the backdrop for unforgettable matches.

    From local club tournaments to international-standard practice sessions, HCC delivers an unmatched experience for players of every level.

    What We Offer

    World-Class
    Facilities

    🏏

    Professional Pitches

    Multiple well-maintained turf and matting pitches designed to international standards — giving bowlers and batters the surface they deserve.

    🔦

    Floodlit Arenas

    State-of-the-art LED floodlights illuminate the ground for evening and night matches, extending your game time well past sunset.

    🎯

    Practice Nets

    Dedicated batting and bowling nets for intensive practice sessions — perfect for honing technique before the big match.

    🏆

    Tournament Hosting

    Fully equipped to host T20 leagues, corporate tournaments, and school championships — with scoring systems and PA facilities.

    🧘

    Changing Rooms

    Spacious, modern changing rooms and player lounge areas ensure comfort before, during, and after every match.

    ☕

    Pavilion & Café

    Relax in our covered pavilion with a refreshment café serving snacks and beverages — the perfect spot for spectators and players alike.
